### 4.1 Vendor Approval Workflow
**Endpoints Verified**:
- ‚úÖ `POST /api/vendor/apply` - Submit vendor application (creates PENDING vendor)
- ‚úÖ `GET /api/vendor/status` - Check application status
- ‚úÖ `POST /api/admin/vendors/[id]/approve` - Approve vendor (adds VENDOR role)
- ‚úÖ `POST /api/admin/vendors/[id]/reject` - Reject vendor (with reason)

4. Admin rejects ‚Üí Status: REJECTED + 30-day cooldown
5. Rejected vendors can reapply after 30 days

**Features**:
- ‚úÖ Duplicate business name check
- ‚úÖ Rejection cooldown period
- ‚úÖ Audit logging
- ‚úÖ Role management

- ‚úÖ `POST /api/admin/stores/[id]/approve` - Approve store (status: ACTIVE)
- ‚úÖ `POST /api/admin/stores/[id]/reject` - Reject store (status: SUSPENDED)
- ‚úÖ `GET /api/admin/stores/pending` - List pending stores

**Workflow**:
1. Vendor creates store ‚Üí Status: PENDING
